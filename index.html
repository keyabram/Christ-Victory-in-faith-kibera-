Rev. Overseer Moses Adie

Pastoral Care – Offering counseling, support, and encouragement to church members in times of joy and crisis. Administration – Managing church affairs, overseeing programs, and making key decisions in collaboration with church leaders. Sacraments and Rituals – Conducting baptisms, weddings, funerals, and Holy Communion services. Community Outreach – Engaging in charity work, supporting the needy, and fostering relationships within the community. Mentorship and Discipleship – Training and mentoring church leaders, youth, and new believers in faith and Christian values. Conflict Resolution – Mediating disputes among church members and maintaining harmony within the church. Teaching and Evangelism – Spreading the Gospel through Bible study sessions, missionary work, and outreach programs. Financial Stewardship – Ensuring proper use of church funds, overseeing donations, and promoting financial transparency. Intercessory Role – Praying for individuals, the church, and society, acting as a spiritual intercessor..
